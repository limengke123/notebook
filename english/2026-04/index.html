    underrated (adjective)¶

    Added: 2026-04-20

    Mandarin: 被低估的 French: sous-estimé(e)

    Definition: Considered less impressive, valuable, or talented than one actually is; not given enough credit or appreciation.

    Examples: - 🇺🇸 This album is criminally underrated — it should have gone platinum. - 🇨🇳 这张专辑被严重低估了，它本该大卖的。 - 🇫🇷 Cet album est vraiment sous-estimé — il aurait dû cartonner.

    Phrases & Collocations: - underrated gem: 被低估的瑰宝（常指影视、音乐、餐厅等） - criminally underrated: 严重被低估的（语气强烈） - underrated talent: 被低估的才能 - underrated skill: 常被忽视的技能

    underestimate (verb / noun)¶

    Added: 2026-04-20

    Mandarin: 低估 French: sous-estimer (v.) / sous-estimation (n.)

    Definition: To think of something or someone as being smaller, less important, or less capable than they actually are. As a noun: an estimate that is too low.

    Examples: - 🇺🇸 Never underestimate how long a meeting can run. - 🇨🇳 永远不要低估一个会议能拖多久。 - 🇫🇷 Ne sous-estimez jamais la durée d'une réunion.

    Phrases & Collocations: - underestimate the risk: 低估风险 - underestimate someone: 小看某人（常含"你会后悔的"语气） - gross underestimate: 严重低估（名词形式） - vastly underestimate: 大大低估

    frictionless (adjective)¶

    Added: 2026-04-20

    Mandarin: 顺畅的；无阻碍的 French: fluide; sans friction; sans accroc

    Definition: Smooth and effortless, with no unnecessary obstacles or steps. Widely used in tech, product, and business contexts to describe seamless user experiences.

    Examples: - 🇺🇸 The goal is to make onboarding as frictionless as possible for new users. - 🇨🇳 我们的目标是让新用户的注册流程尽可能顺畅无阻。 - 🇫🇷 L'objectif est de rendre l'intégration aussi fluide que possible pour les nouveaux utilisateurs.

    Phrases & Collocations: - frictionless experience: 无缝体验（产品设计常用词） - frictionless onboarding: 顺畅的新用户引导流程 - frictionless payments: 无感支付；免密支付 - frictionless collaboration: 高效顺畅的协作

    overwhelmed (adjective)¶

    Added: 2026-04-21

    Mandarin: 不堪重负的；不知所措的；（情感上）被淹没的 French: dépassé(e); submergé(e); accablé(e)

    Definition: Feeling unable to manage or process everything coming at you — tasks, emotions, news, or sensory input — often to the point of stress or paralysis.

    Examples: - 🇺🇸 I was so overwhelmed by emails that I missed the deadline. - 🇨🇳 邮件多得我快招架不住了，结果错过了截止日期。 - 🇫🇷 J'étais tellement dépassé par les e-mails que j'ai raté la date limite.

    Phrases & Collocations: - feel overwhelmed: 感到不堪重负 - overwhelmed with joy/gratitude: （正面）喜不自胜 / 感激不尽 - overwhelmed by choice: 选择太多反而无从下手

    property (noun)¶

    Added: 2026-04-21

    Mandarin: 财产；不动产；特性；（编程）属性 French: propriété (f.)

    Definition: Something owned (land, buildings, goods); a quality or characteristic that belongs to a thing; in programming, a named field or accessor on an object.

    Examples: - 🇺🇸 They invested in rental property downtown. - 🇨🇳 他们在市中心买了几套出租房做投资。 - 🇫🇷 Ils ont investi dans des biens immobiliers à louer en centre-ville.

    Phrases & Collocations: - intellectual property: 知识产权 - private property: 私有财产 - material properties: 材料性能 / 材料属性 - read-only property (computing): 只读属性

    intellectual (adjective / noun)¶

    Added: 2026-04-21

    Mandarin: 智力的；用脑力的；知识分子的（名词：知识分子） French: intellectuel(le)

    Definition: Relating to serious thinking, ideas, or scholarship. As a noun: someone whose work centers on ideas, culture, or academia (sometimes with a slightly detached stereotype).

    Examples: - 🇺🇸 The debate stayed civil but deeply intellectual. - 🇨🇳 整场辩论很克制，但讨论层次很高、很“烧脑”。 - 🇫🇷 Le débat est resté courtois mais profondément intellectuel.

    Phrases & Collocations: - intellectual curiosity: 求知欲；好奇心（偏学术） - intellectual honesty: 学术/讨论上的诚实（承认反例与局限） - public intellectual: 公共知识分子

    violation (noun)¶

    Added: 2026-04-21

    Mandarin: 违反；违背；侵犯（权利） French: violation (f.); infraction (f.)

    Definition: An act that breaks a law, rule, contract, or norm; can also mean an infringement of someone's rights, privacy, or bodily integrity.

    Examples: - 🇺🇸 Speeding is a traffic violation, not a felony. - 🇨🇳 超速属于交通违章，不是重罪。 - 🇫🇷 L'excès de vitesse est une infraction au code de la route, pas un crime.

    Phrases & Collocations: - human rights violation: 侵犯人权 - terms-of-service violation: 违反服务条款 - in violation of: 违反……（法律/合同/政策）
