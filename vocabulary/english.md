## underrated (adjective)
> *Added: 2026-04-20*

**Mandarin:** 被低估的 (bèi dīgū de)
**French:** sous-estimé(e)

**Definition:** Considered less impressive, valuable, or talented than one actually is; not given enough credit or appreciation.

**Examples:**
- 🇺🇸 This album is criminally underrated — it should have gone platinum.
- 🇨🇳 这张专辑被严重低估了，它本该大卖的。
- 🇫🇷 Cet album est vraiment sous-estimé — il aurait dû cartonner.

**Phrases & Collocations:**
- underrated gem: 被低估的瑰宝（常指影视、音乐、餐厅等）
- criminally underrated: 严重被低估的（语气强烈）
- underrated talent: 被低估的才能
- underrated skill: 常被忽视的技能

## underestimate (verb / noun)
> *Added: 2026-04-20*

**Mandarin:** 低估 (dīgū)
**French:** sous-estimer (v.) / sous-estimation (n.)

**Definition:** To think of something or someone as being smaller, less important, or less capable than they actually are. As a noun: an estimate that is too low.

**Examples:**
- 🇺🇸 Never underestimate how long a meeting can run.
- 🇨🇳 永远不要低估一个会议能拖多久。
- 🇫🇷 Ne sous-estimez jamais la durée d'une réunion.

**Phrases & Collocations:**
- underestimate the risk: 低估风险
- underestimate someone: 小看某人（常含"你会后悔的"语气）
- gross underestimate: 严重低估（名词形式）
- vastly underestimate: 大大低估

## frictionless (adjective)
> *Added: 2026-04-20*

**Mandarin:** 顺畅的；无阻碍的 (shùnchàng de; wú zǔ'ài de)
**French:** fluide; sans friction; sans accroc

**Definition:** Smooth and effortless, with no unnecessary obstacles or steps. Widely used in tech, product, and business contexts to describe seamless user experiences.

**Examples:**
- 🇺🇸 The goal is to make onboarding as frictionless as possible for new users.
- 🇨🇳 我们的目标是让新用户的注册流程尽可能顺畅无阻。
- 🇫🇷 L'objectif est de rendre l'intégration aussi fluide que possible pour les nouveaux utilisateurs.

**Phrases & Collocations:**
- frictionless experience: 无缝体验（产品设计常用词）
- frictionless onboarding: 顺畅的新用户引导流程
- frictionless payments: 无感支付；免密支付
- frictionless collaboration: 高效顺畅的协作
